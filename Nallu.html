Active Volcanoes

Introduction

There are approximately 500 active volcanoes in the world today, not including those underneath the oceans.
In fact, as you read these words, there are probably 20 volcanoes erupting right now.
Between 50-70 volcanoes are erupting every year, 160 have erupted in the last decade.
And there are about 550 that have erupted since the beginning of recorded history.
